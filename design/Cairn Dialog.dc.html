Cairn · Dialog
One reusable confirm dialog for the whole app, replacing Flutter's Material AlertDialog. A dimmed backdrop, a parchment card, an optional round icon, a title (Zilla Slab), a muted body line, and two actions: a secondary "Cancel" and a primary confirm. The confirm has two tones: sage for ordinary/affirmative confirms (Sign out), clay for destructive ones (Delete). Tapping the backdrop or Cancel dismisses it; only the confirm runs the action.

Reusable API
showCairnDialog takes: an optional round icon, a title, a body, a cancel label (default "Cancel"), a confirm label, and a confirm tone (sage default, or clay for destructive). Returns whether the user confirmed. Dismiss on backdrop tap, Cancel, or system back = not confirmed.
First use: the Profile "Sign out" confirmation (Frame 1), using the clay (evident) tone so the action stands out even though it is not destructive. Frame 2 shows the same clay tone on a genuinely destructive confirm; it is not wired to anything yet. The sage tone stays available for gentle/affirmative confirms.
Card: parchment gradient #f4efe4 to #e6decb, 24px radius, top-bevel highlight, deep soft shadow. Scrim: rgba(30,25,18,.52). Title 22px Zilla Slab 700 #2c2924. Body 14px #6a6153. Buttons 14px pad, 14px radius; Cancel is bordered parchment, confirm is a filled sage or clay gradient.
